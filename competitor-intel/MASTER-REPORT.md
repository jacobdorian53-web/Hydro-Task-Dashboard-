## Creative Strategy Analysis

### 1. The "Impossible Math" Hook (Primary Pattern)
Their top-performing creatives use absurd nutritional comparisons:

> "1 glass of hydrogen water has the same antioxidant effect as:
> - 516 apples
> - 1 kg of blueberries
> - 38 carrots
> - 5 kg of broccoli
> - 30 glasses of green tea"

**Why it works:**
- Specific numbers create credibility (516 > "lots of")
- Familiar "superfoods" validate unfamiliar tech
- Creates "shortcut to health" positioning
- Pattern interrupt stops scrolling

### 2. Offer Strategy
- **Heavy discounting:** 45-50% off consistently
- **Urgency:** "Limited Time," "New Year Sale"
- **Risk reversal:** 90-day guarantee prominently featured
- **Social proof:** "162,821+ happy customers"

### 3. Visual Language
- Product hero shot at center
- Natural wood/greenery backgrounds
- Blue-tinted water (signals hydrogen/purity)
- Active bubbling effect (product "working")
- Premium glass + stainless steel aesthetic

### 4. Creative Mix

| Video | 1:1 | 47s | Feed placement |
