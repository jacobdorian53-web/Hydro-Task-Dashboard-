📋 Creative Brief: Comparison Hook Ad

Priority 1

Concept Overview

Hook: "1 Hydro Bottle = 516 apples worth of antioxidants"

Format: Static image (1:1, 4:5, 9:16 variants)

Funnel Position: TOFU (Awareness)

Objective: Landing page views, engagement

Visual Requirements

  • Hydro Go bottle centered, glowing effect
  • Radial arrangement of food comparisons
  • Natural wood/greenery background
  • Clean white typography
  • Brand colors: Navy blue (#1a365d), accent blue (#3182ce)
  • Performance

Copy

Primary Text:

"1 Hydro Bottle has the same antioxidant effect as:"
