Our targeting strategy follows a full-funnel approach, with specific audiences for each stage of the customer journey.

## Exclusion Strategy

### Always Exclude
- Purchasers (last 7 days) from prospecting
- Email unsubscribers
- Previous returns/refunds
- Competitors (if identifiable)

### Conditional Exclusions
- Exclude existing customers from TOFU (optional)
- Exclude recent site visitors from broad prospecting

## Testing Framework

### Audience Tests to Run

**Week 1-2: Interest Stacks**
- Test: Health vs Fitness vs Lifestyle
- Budget: $50/day per stack
- Success metric: CPA, CTR

**Week 3-4: Lookalike Sizes**
- Test: 1% vs 2% vs 3%
- Budget: $50/day per size
- Success metric: CPA, scale potential

**Week 5-6: Niche Audiences**
- Test: Athletes vs Biohackers vs Anti-aging
- Budget: $30/day per niche
- Success metric: CTR, engagement, ROAS

### Scaling Rules

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| CPA < target for 3 days | Increase budget 20% |
| CPA > target for 3 days | Decrease budget 30% |
| ROAS > 2.0x | Scale aggressively |
| ROAS 1.5-2.0x | Scale slowly |
| ROAS < 1.5x | Optimize or pause |
