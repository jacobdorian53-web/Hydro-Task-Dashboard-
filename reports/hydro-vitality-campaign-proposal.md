## Creative Concepts

### Concept 1: "The Math Ad" (TOFU)

**Hook:** "1 Hydro Bottle = [absurd comparison]"

**Variations to test:**
- "1 bottle = 516 apples worth of antioxidants"
- "1 bottle = 38 carrots of nutrition"
- "1 bottle = 30 cups of green tea"

**Visual:** Product centered, radial food comparison, natural backdrop

**CTA:** "See the science" → Science page

---

### Concept 2: "The Shortcut" (TOFU)

**Hook:** "Stop eating 5kg of broccoli every day"

**Script outline:**
- Problem: Getting enough antioxidants is exhausting
- Agitation: You'd need to eat impossible amounts
- Solution: One button, hydrogen water, done

**Visual:** Split screen - pile of vegetables vs. Hydro bottle

**CTA:** "Upgrade your water" → Product page

---

### Concept 3: "The Athlete" (TOFU - Niche)

**Hook:** "Why pro athletes are switching to hydrogen water"

**Angles:**
- Recovery time reduction
- Inflammation control
- Performance edge

**Visual:** Athletic lifestyle, workout context, bottle in gym bag

**CTA:** "Train smarter" → Product page

---

### Concept 4: "The Offer" (MOFU)

**Hook:** "50% off ends tonight"

**Elements:**
- Countdown urgency
- Triple product display
- Social proof ("3,400+ 5-star reviews")
- Risk reversal ("90-day guarantee")

**Visual:** Clean product shots, sale badge, timer

**CTA:** "Shop the sale" → Collection page

---

### Concept 5: "The Stack" (MOFU - Bundle Focus)

**Hook:** "The Essential Kit: Everything you need for $139"

**Value stack:**
- Hydro Go bottle ($99 value)
- Travel case ($29 value)
- Extra filters ($49 value)
- = $177 value for $139

**Visual:** Kit components spread, then assembled

**CTA:** "Get the kit" → Bundle page

---

### Concept 6: "The Testimonial" (BOFU)

**Hook:** "I was skeptical too..."

**Structure:**
- Skepticism opener (relatable)
- First week experience
- 30-day transformation
- Recommendation

**Visual:** UGC-style, authentic, phone-recorded feel

**CTA:** "Try risk-free" → Product with guarantee emphasis

## Funnel Structure

```
TOFU (Awareness) - 60% of spend
├── Math comparison ads
├── Shortcut/convenience angle
├── Niche targeting (athletes, biohackers)
└── Goal: Landing page views, engagement

MOFU (Consideration) - 30% of spend
├── Offer/sale ads
├── Bundle value ads
├── Retargeting site visitors
└── Goal: Add to cart, initiate checkout

BOFU (Conversion) - 10% of spend
├── Testimonial/UGC ads
├── Abandoned cart retargeting
├── Risk reversal emphasis
└── Goal: Purchase
```
