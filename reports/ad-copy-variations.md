## Emoji Usage Guide

**Do use:**
- 🍎 (comparisons)
- ✅ (checklists/benefits)
- ⭐ (reviews)
- ⏰ (urgency)
- 💧 (product/water)

**Avoid:**
- Excessive emojis (max 3 per ad)
- Emojis in headlines
- Random decorative emojis
